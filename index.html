I am Waleed and I want to be a software engineer. I want to experience the thrill of creating and completing development of an application. And to get there I want to start early and complete classes here at Dublin high so I can get a head start. This is my career choice because computing systems in general intrigue me, and since we are in the digital age it is only becoming more interesting.

This year, my Junior year, I am two years ahead in math. On top of that I am taking AP Computer Science Applications. I used to wrestle, run in cross country, and play basketball, but that was before the pandemic. I am determined to become the best version of myself.

I have done many things to achieve my goal of becoming a software engineer. But no one said it stops there, another goal is to combine software engineering with business, and I know I can do it. With the resources provided to me here at Dublin High, I am sure to make my dreams come true.
